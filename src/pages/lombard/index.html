Business Partners

Fortune 200 companies that possess their own real-estate holding

Lombard creates partnerships with an exclusive clientele list that is solely comprised of Fortune 200 companies. In most instances, Lombard's clients possess their own real-estate holding operating within the continental United States. Companies on this list include: Public Storage, Guardian Life Insurance Company, McDonald’s, and CVS.

The partnership is based on a three-point service that provides a comprehensive service that supports the logistical demands of our clients in order to adequately manage an extensive number of commercial facilities in a simultaneous manner.

About Us

belief statement

Our secret to success has been to do things the Lombard Way!

Lombard defines professionalism and integrity in commercial contracting & facility management simply as: (1) being available for our clients to address needs without prior notice; (2) providing carefully crafted estimates; (3) completing work to a high degree of quality; (4) documenting our work by providing before, during and after pictures for projects; (5) remaining engaged with our clients as a fully integrated partner throughout the project lifecycle by returning to a project and/or working through issues that may arise; (6) and last, but certainly not least, the little important things such as: answering the telephone and responding to email correspondences in a timely fashion.


what is lombard?

Procuring services to replace the a 100,000 square foot parking lot or painting the entire 60,000 square foot building entails a bit more than a simple Google search on the internet. The processes are vast and meticulous. Our procurement professional(s) are trained at the highest levels in the field, and routinely and contemporaneously successfully manage a multitude of projects from cradle to grave.

Lombard Exteriors is an emerging leader in the corporate real estate support market. Lombard provides facility management, procurement and general contracting services to its clients. Lombard is currently engaged in projects in the following states: New York, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Georgia, Florida, Rhode Island, Tennessee and Ohio. The portfolio of properties Lombard supports grew from 47 in 2015 to over 300 by the end of 2018.
